about us

S.S Global Limited, is a wholly Ghanaian and privately owned Limited liability company incorporated in Ghana. A rare gem of Ghanaian entrepreneurship. S.S Global Limited has built unsurpassed services, unmatched skills and unprecedented quality through a visionary and innovation management team headed by a creative goal driven young energetic CEO who sees beyond the traditional way of doing things. From its humble beginnings but with the passion for quality and success, we have developed into modern, multi-crafted and multi-talented organisation with six years of vast experiences in all phases of professional Fencing and Construction. Our reputation for excellence is based on versatility and professionalism, working in close partnership with our clients and stakeholders. We select from a wide range of options the most viable solution to each new challenge. We are noted for our determination to maintain the highest levels of professionalism and our proven ability to execute projects effectively on schedule, regardless of size or complexity. Our projects are handled by a highly skilled, motivated professionalism and a team of professional staff. We have created an environment that attracts to notch, highly innovative, experienced and qualified Ghanaians who see every job as a new challenge and opportunity to improve upon their, skill expertise and experience.
